TabGroup Master — Ultimate Workspace Manager

Organize, restore, and automate your Chrome tab groups with precision.

Import your saved groups — First Time Setup

  1. Run NativeHost/install.bat once (registers the helper and installs its one dependency).
  2. Open the side panel and click Import from Chrome.
  3. All your saved tab groups are read directly from Chrome's local data in a couple of seconds — no tabs opened, no clicking, nothing loaded into memory. Re-run anytime; only new groups are added.
